Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Saint Benedict Center West is a small religious community. We collect only the information you voluntarily provide — such as your name and email address when subscribing to our newsletter — and we use it solely to communicate with you about our community and apostolate.

Information We Collect

We collect your name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter. We do not collect any other personal data through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to send our newsletter and occasional correspondence from our houses. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your information with any third party.

Your Rights

You may request removal from our mailing list at any time by writing to us at the address listed on the Contact page.

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please write to us by mail at Saint Benedict Center West, Box 660771, Arcadia, CA 91066.
